##Show your hidden files
1. open your terminal
2. input `defaults write com.apple.finder AppleShowAllFiles YES`
then `killall Finder`

##Hide your hidden files
1. open your terminal
2. input `defaults write com.apple.finder AppleShowAllFiles NO`
then `killall Finder`
